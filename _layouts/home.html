Introducing: Streamboxx

Your all-in-one solution for simple live streaming and archiving of spoken-word events.

Features

Plug-And-Play

No setup necessary. It works right out of the box.

Intelligent

Can automatically begin and end streams, removing dead air at the beginning and end of a talk.

Scalable

You can control up to 25 stages from one computer.

Reliable

Even if the Streamboxx loses connection to the internet, it always makes a backup recording.

Helpful

The Streamboxx automatically saves the talk to the Voice Republic archive.
